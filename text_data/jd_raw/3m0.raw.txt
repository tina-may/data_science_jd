The Impact You’ll Make in this Role
As a Computational Linguist, you will have the opportunity to tap into your curiosity and collaborate with some of the most innovative and diverse people around the world. Here, you will make an impact by:

Supporting clinical Natural Language Understanding (NLU) system by maintaining and improving grammars, parsers, and terminologies
Extracting, manipulating, and analyzing textual data and annotations using Python and batch scripts
Determining solutions and priorities for NLU issues and enhancement requests
Implementing and testing solutions in coordination with clinical, technical, and business teams
Your Skills and Expertise  
To set you up for success in this role from day one, 3M requires (at a minimum) the following qualifications:

Bachelor’s Degree or higher in Linguistics AND/OR Computational Linguistics (completed and verified prior to start) from an accredited institution
OR

High School Diploma/GED (completed and verified prior to start) and eight (8) years of experience in grammars and parsing, corpus analysis
Additional qualifications that could help you succeed even further in this role include:

Experience with grammars and parsing, corpus analysis
Experience with data annotation AND/OR medical coding and documentation.
Experience with regular expressions
Experience in software development (course work and internship experience considered)
Master’s degree in a Linguistics or related field with an emphasis on computational linguistics, corpus analysis, syntax/morphology or related
Experience with Python
Experience/education with medical coding and documentation or healthcare informatics
Proficiency with regular expressions
Experience handling/transforming different formats including HTML, XML, JSON
Experience with GitHub (merge/resolve conflicts) or other version control
Experience with Java
Good analytical and troubleshooting skills
